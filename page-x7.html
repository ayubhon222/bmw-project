    Front end desing.

    The front part of the body with an emphasis on branded BMW design elements emphasizes the monumentality and sporty character of the car. The double-lit radiator grille with BMW Iconic Glow creates a sense of progressiveness. The intricate double slats of the grille, combined with exclusive chrome accents, are a real eye-catcher.

    The combination of the athletic edges of the BMW X7 silhouette and the new color Sparkling Copper Gray (metallic gray) gives the SUV a dynamic and elegant look. And 23" BMW Individual light alloy wheels enhance the feeling of power. One look at the car is enough to understand that it has an incredibly spacious interior.

    The new BMW X7 looks ultra-modern thanks to the three-dimensional design of the rear lights. A filigree, chrome-plated glass trim connects the taillights, emphasizing exceptional quality. This design solution emphasizes the width of the car body, its power and elegance.
